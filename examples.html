Multiple Category Groups:

Selecting multiple categories within one group will show you items that match at least one of the selected categories. For example, selecting "Category 1" and "Category 2" will show all items that are in either of those categories.

Selecting categories from multiple groups will show you items that match at least one of the potential filter combinations. For example, selecting "Category 1", "Category 2", and "Price 3" will show all items in "Category 1" and "Price 3" or "Category 2" and "Price 3".
